Community Guidelines

Terms & Conditions

Our goal is to build a safe, supportive, and effective community. Please read these guidelines to understand how our volunteer network operates.

1. Platform Purpose & Capabilities

Seva Connect is built on a foundation of compassion and community service. We operate purely as a facilitation platform—a bridge designed to connect individuals in need with kind-hearted local volunteers. While our entire team works tirelessly to coordinate relief, blood donations, and support, we are a civilian network. Therefore, we cannot absolutely guarantee the immediate availability of volunteers, resources, or real-time assistance in every single situation.

2. Critical Emergency Disclaimer

Seva Connect does not, and cannot, replace official emergency services. If you or someone you know is experiencing a life-threatening medical emergency, a severe crisis, or requires immediate rescue operations, you must always contact official authorities first (such as dialing 108 for Ambulance or 100 for Police). Our volunteers are here to provide secondary, on-the-ground community support only after official professionals have been notified.

3. Volunteer Code of Conduct

By registering as a volunteer on Seva Connect, you agree to act with the highest level of integrity, respect, and empathy. Volunteers must never charge fees or request compensation for the help they provide through this platform. Any breach of trust, misuse of user data, or inappropriate behavior will result in immediate removal from the network.

4. Limitation of Liability

Because we rely on the goodwill of independent community members, Seva Connect cannot be held legally liable for the actions of individual users, the exact timing of responses, or the outcomes of coordinated efforts. We provide this technology in good faith to uplift society, and users engage with the platform understanding these limitations.

Last Updated: June 2026. By continuing to use Seva Connect, you agree to these terms and our shared mission of community care.
