Privacy Policy

At Seva Connect, we respect your privacy and are committed to protecting the personal information you share with us. This policy outlines how we handle your data.

1. Information We Collect

When you interact with Seva Connect—whether by volunteering, requesting assistance, or contacting us—we may collect the following personal information: your full name, phone number, email address, physical location, and the specific details of your request. We only collect information that you voluntarily submit through our secure digital forms.

2. How We Use Your Data

The sole purpose of collecting this information is to facilitate humanitarian coordination. Specifically, we use your data to:
• Connect those in need with verified local volunteers. • Respond to your inquiries and support requests. • Organize and manage community events and awareness campaigns.

3. Data Sharing & Protection

We will never sell, rent, or trade your personal information to third-party marketers or advertisers. Your details are shared strictly internally, and only with the specific verified volunteers necessary to fulfill your request. All data is securely processed and stored using industry-standard encrypted databases (such as Firebase) to prevent unauthorized access.

4. Your Rights

You have the right to request access to the personal data we hold about you, or to request that we permanently delete your information from our systems. If you wish to have your data removed, please contact us directly at sevaconnectofficial@gmail.com.

Last Updated: June 2026. This policy applies to all services and platforms operated by Seva Connect.
