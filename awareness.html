Education

The Awareness Hub

Knowledge is power. Browse our curated library of community education topics to learn how you can make a meaningful impact in your daily life.

Environment

Environmental Protection

The Barak Valley's ecosystem is delicate. Understanding our role in protecting forests, water bodies, and biodiversity is crucial. Simple actions like reducing single-use plastics and participating in local clean-up drives can drastically reduce pollution and restore natural habitats for future generations.

Health

Blood Donation

Every single donation can save up to three lives. Blood cannot be manufactured; it can only come from generous donors. Regular blood donation not only saves patients facing surgery, trauma, or chronic illnesses, but it also improves the cardiovascular health of the donor.

Wellbeing

Mental Health

Mental health is just as important as physical health. Breaking the stigma in our community starts with encouraging open, compassionate conversations. If you or someone you know is struggling with anxiety or depression, reaching out to local support groups or verified counselors is the first step toward healing.

Disaster Relief

Flood Preparedness

Our region is prone to heavy flooding. Being prepared saves lives. Always keep an emergency kit ready with first-aid, flashlights, non-perishable food, and essential documents in waterproof bags. During floods, follow community leaders and utilize Seva Connect's volunteer network for safe evacuation routes.
